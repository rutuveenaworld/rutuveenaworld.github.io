About the Destination
Karnataka is a unique combination of historic culture and contemporary attractions! The state is also one of the leading hubs for Information Technology and sports some of the best hill stations of Southern India. Explore the wonderful cities of Bangalore, Coorg, Mysore and Hassan on this tour!

Things to Do in Karnataka:
  • Madikeri Fort
  • Famous Buddhist Monastery and the Golden Buddha Temple
  • Mysore Palace
Best Time to Visit Karnataka
October through April is the best time to visit Karnataka and South India. Owing to the location of the state in South India, the climate of Karnataka remains on the warmer side throughout the year. Winters are mild, summer are hot and humid, and monsoon arrives early in Karnataka.
