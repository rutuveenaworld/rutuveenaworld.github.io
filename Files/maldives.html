 About the Destination
The Maldives is officially known as the Republic of Maldives. This Southeast Asian island nation is located in the Arabian Sea of the Indian Ocean. The Maldives was initially a sultanate after Islam was introduced here in the 12th century. By the 16th century, it had come under European colonial rule..
