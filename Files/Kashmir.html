About the Destination
Jammu and Kashmir is the northernmost territory of the Indian state and is an amalgamation of 3 very different cultures and geographies. Jammu is located on the southern edge of the region and is full of green pastures and picturesque sights. Jammu Airport is located at a distance of 8 km from the city centre, while Jammu Tawi is an important railhead in the region. The region is also well-known for being the abode of the renowned Vaishno Devi Temple.

Experience Kashmir
When you visit with a suitable Jammu and Kashmir tour package, you will be able to witness for yourself how the region has been a hot spot for various cultures to develop, due to which there is a vast difference of cultures in this small geographic location. The southern-most region, Jammu is a hub for the Hindu faith, with Kashmiri Pandits being the original inhabitants of the region.
