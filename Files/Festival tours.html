Festivals Holiday


  Some days are chaotic, and some are frustrating. Then there are some when people forget all their worries and come together to celebrate the true spirit of life. This is what festivals around the world do to you. They infuse positivity and cheer into an otherwise stressful life.
   With thousands of cultures around the world, some major some minor, each culture has its unique way of commemorating the efforts of their ancestors and preserving the legacy for future generations. There are thousands of ways people around the world celebrate festivals during festival vacations, but the underlying current remains the same – to forge camaraderie and kinship and spread happiness.
